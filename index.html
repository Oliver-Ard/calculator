Calculator Keyboard Guide

Simple calculator with keyboard support.

You can use the Numpad keys, as well as keys on the left side of the keyboard.

  • Numbers: Numpad[0-9] and Digit[0-9];
  • Plus Sign(+): NumpadAdd and KeyA;
  • Minus Sign(-): NumpadSubtract and KeyS;
  • Multiplication Sign(×): NumpadMultiply and KeyD;
  • Division Sign(÷): NumpadDivide and KeyF;
  • Equal Sign(=): NumpadDivide and KeyG;
  • Percent sign(%): KeyQ;
  • Period(.): KeyW;
  • Negation(+/−): KeyE;
  • Delete(DEL): KeyR;
  • All Clear(AC): KeyT;

You can click the i or press KeyI button if you need to come back for the keyboard instructions.
